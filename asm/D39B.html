D39B: Reads In-Game Keyboard Controls (1-2-3)
Used by the routine at Controls_Joystick.
Controls_Keyboard D39B LD A,$F7 Read from the keyboard;
Port Number Bit
0 1 2 3 4
F7 1 2 3 4 5
