CE12: Control Method Routine Alias.
Points to either CONTROLS_JOYSTICK or CONTROLS_KEYBOARD depending on the chosen control method.
CONTROL_ROUTINE_ALIAS CE12 DEFB $9B,$D3 16 bit memory location of either the keyboard or joystick control routines.
